Michael C. Hall stars in this crime thriller series as Dexter Morgan, a likable Miami forensics expert who hides a shocking secret: he's a vigilante serial killer using his skills and knowledge to murder criminals who have slipped through the cracks of the justice system and avoid detection. Jennifer Carpenter, Lauren Velez, Julie Benz, Erik King, David Zayas and James Remar costar in this series based on the novel "Darkly Dreaming Dexter" by Jeff Lindsay.
