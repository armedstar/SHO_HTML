Michael C. Hall

Born: Raleigh, North Carolina

Michael C. Hall studied at Earlham College before relocating to NYC to enroll in NYU's Master of Fine Arts program in acting. He spent his early career on stage and has starred in such well-known productions as off-Broadway's "Corpus Christi" and Broadway's "Cabaret." Hall made his feature film debut in 2003 as Agent Klein in the John Woo thriller "Paycheck." He has since starred in many well-received roles, including funeral director David Fisher in the series "Six Feet Under" and serial killer Dexter Morgan on the Showtime series "Dexter."
